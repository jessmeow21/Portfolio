I am Jessica Sulaiman

I've studied music and video production during my time in college and currently do so in my spare time at home. I've transferred from Community College of Philadelphia to Temple University in 2013. I graduated from Temple in 2015.
I've caught an interest in Web Development that is why I'm currently attending a coding bootcamp at the University of Pennsylvania and I'm thrilled to have the opportunity to learn new languages. This is certainly an area that interests me to continue to learn and apply in my projects.
My philosophy is that life is a journey, we should remain as curious as we were as kids and we shouldn't take it too seriously, however, responsibly of course!
At this moment, I'm grateful for everything that I have come across, the good, the bad, and the ugly. I'm excited about the new opportunities that lie ahead with full conviction and positivity.

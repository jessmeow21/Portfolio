About Me

I am Jessica Maria Sulaiman and I'm from Philadelphia, PA USA.

Not that long ago did I catch an interest in Web Development. It's an interesting area of tech to me and I felt a deep need to improve myself. I figured the best way to help myself get into the field was to attend a coding bootcamp at the University of Pennsylvania. There, I got my toes wet and it kept my interest even through the frustrating parts of learning new languages in the pace of this course. Now that I'm quite in a good level of expertise, I'm confident in my abilities to complete web development projects with satisfaction for the user in mind.

At heart, I enjoy being creative and curious. Right now I'm onto the next project to help a business or improve someone's online visibility, whatever the case may be, I'm happy to contribute my coding skills and design expertise.
